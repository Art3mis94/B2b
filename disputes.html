How Disputes Are Handled

To maintain fairness and transparency, all funds related to a transaction remain securely held in escrow until the dispute is resolved. Our process ensures both buyers and sellers are protected.

Step 1: Direct Communication

  1. Buyers and sellers are encouraged to resolve issues directly within 3 business days.
  2. Funds remain frozen in escrow during this period.

Step 2: Marketplace Mediation

  1. If no resolution is reached, a formal dispute can be submitted by email.
  2. Include all supporting information so our team can review efficiently.

Step 3: Resolution & Release

  1. Funds are released according to the mediation outcome.
  2. An appeal may be submitted once with additional evidence if necessary.

Submit a Dispute via Email

To submit a dispute, please email disputes@b2bsurplus.com with the following information:

  • Item ID: Include the unique ID of the listing.
  • Item Name: Clearly state the name of the item.
  • Description of Issue: Explain the problem (e.g., item not delivered, incorrect quantity, damaged item).
  • Supporting Evidence: Attach any relevant photos, screenshots, or documents to substantiate your claim.

Our team will review your submission and contact you within 2–3 business days with next steps. Funds will remain in escrow until the dispute is resolved.
