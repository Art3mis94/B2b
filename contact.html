General Inquiries

If you have a question, feedback, or general inquiry, our support team is happy to assist you. Please email us with the details of your request.

Email: support@b2bsurplus.com

Please include the following when relevant:

  • Your Name and Business Name
  • Reason for contacting us (e.g., technical support, listing question, general inquiry)
  • Item ID / Listing reference if your query is about a specific listing

Our team will respond within 1–2 business days.

Raise a Dispute

If a transaction has an issue, please submit a dispute via email. Funds remain securely held in escrow until the matter is resolved.

Email: disputes@b2bsurplus.com

Include the following details:

  • Item ID: The unique ID of the listing.
  • Item Name: Clearly state the name of the item.
  • Description of Issue: Explain the problem (e.g., not delivered, incorrect quantity, damaged item).
  • Supporting Evidence: Attach photos, screenshots, or documents to substantiate your claim.

Our team will review your dispute and contact you within 2–3 business days with the next steps.
