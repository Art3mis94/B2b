How It Works

Simple, secure, and efficient B2B surplus trading in 3 steps.

Trading Process

1

List Your Surplus

Create a free account and add your unused inventory with photos, specs, and fulfillment options. Set your price and publish instantly.

2

Connect & Agree

Buyers browse, message you directly, and agree on terms. Funds are held securely in escrow until delivery is confirmed.
