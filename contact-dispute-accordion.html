Contact & Dispute

Get support or resolve transaction issues quickly and securely.

If you have a question, feedback, or general inquiry, our support team is happy to assist you. Email us with the details of your request.

Email: support@b2bsurplus.com

  • Your Name and Business Name
  • Reason for contacting us (technical support, listing question, general inquiry)
  • Item ID / Listing reference if your query is about a specific listing

Our team will respond within 1–2 business days.

If a transaction has an issue, submit a dispute via email. Funds remain securely held in escrow until the matter is resolved.

  1. Direct Communication: Buyers and sellers are encouraged to resolve issues directly within 3 business days. Funds remain frozen during this period.
  2. Marketplace Mediation: If no resolution is reached, submit a dispute via email with supporting evidence. Our team reviews the details carefully.
  3. Resolution & Release: Funds are released according to the mediation outcome. An appeal may be submitted once with additional evidence if necessary.

Email: disputes@b2bsurplus.com

  • Item ID: Include the unique ID of the listing
  • Item Name: Clearly state the item name
  • Description of Issue: Explain the problem (not delivered, wrong quantity, damaged, etc.)
  • Supporting Evidence: Attach photos, screenshots, or documents

Our team will review your dispute and contact you within 2–3 business days.
