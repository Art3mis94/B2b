How It Works

Upload

List Your Surplus

Upload items with photos, condition, specs, and fulfillment options (collection, seller delivery, or platform logistics).

Search

Browse & Connect

Buyers search by sector, tags, condition, and price. Message sellers directly to negotiate.

Payment

Secure Purchase

Complete payment via secure checkout. Funds held in escrow until delivery confirmed.

Secure Transaction Handling

All payments are held in a secure business escrow account until the item is collected or delivered. Once both parties confirm receipt, funds are released automatically to the seller.

This ensures full transparency, protection, and confidence for buyers and sellers.
